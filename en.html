TLMOTO

About

TLMoto was founded in 2012 and is formed by a team of engineering students from Instituto Superior Técnico, whose main objective is to design, build and run with a sports motorcycle at MotoStudent, an international university competition. At it's current state, the project counts with two built prototypes, a combustion one and an electric one. We also compete in the National Speed Championship (Campeonato Nacional de Velocidade).

Competition

MotoStudent is an engineering competition in which different teams of university students have the opportunity to apply their knowledge in the development of a sports motorcycle. The project is evaluated over several stages and culminates in the Final Event in Aragon, where the prototypes are tested and evaluated.

The TLMoto debuted at MotoStudent 2013-2014, at a time when there was only the category of Combustion. With the birth of the Electric category, the team accepted the challenge of producing a 100% electric prototype, with which it competed in MotoStudent 2017-2018.

Prototypes

TLM01i

TLM01i is a combustion-based motorcycle, incorporated with a 250cc, 4 stroke engine from Sherco. Structurally, the motorcycle is made with a twin spar frame in latticed aluminum. Currently, the bike weighs about 100kg. It has a top speed of approximately 180km/h. This motorcycle has undergone some changes since its participation in Motostudent 2014 for performance improvement, such as a new programmable ECU, new suspensions, among other components.





TLM02e

TLM02e is an electric motorcycle, incorporated with an electric motor with rated power of 15kW, and maximum peak power of 45kW, with a battery pack with approximately 6kWh of power. The structural structure consists of a double frame in lattice aluminum. Currently, the bike weighs about 150kg. It has a maximum output of approximately 170km/h.
